BACKGROUND

AQOON MAAL CONSTRUCTION AND TRANSPORTATION COMPANY - is a professional Construction and Transportation company founded by A business people as multiple Operators after felt the need of business community, Humanitarian organizations and as well as community in Construction and delivering, supplying of provided dispatched goods and commodities, particularly. Our main markets out of the country of services. AQOON MAAL COMPANY was established on 25 August 1st as Construction and Transportation co. - gained rich experience and established stable and credible business relationships with customers within and out of Somalia. With rich experience in dealing all types of the said unique business, including both large and small orders on Construction and import/export of goods and timely provision of service (Procurement, Logistics and Warehousing) we have developed extensive contacts with many local and international organizations working in Somalia. Consequently, we are able to ship (transport) our goods safely and reliably to almost anywhere in Somalia including south central Regions of Somalia "Customers foremost and service first" are our top priorities. We sincerely hope to expand our business through cooperation with individuals and organizations working in all over Somalia.

Business Overview

AQOONMAAL CONSTRUCTION & TRANSPORTATION COMPANY was founded by young entrepreneurs and has expanded significantly over the years thanks to open business guidelines and policies governed by international standards. Our work entails the procurement, Transportation and delivery of goods and services.

ORGANISATION

AQOONMAAL CONSTRUCTION & TRANSPORTATION CO currently operates from two countries; Kenya, and Somalia with four offices in Somalia; Kismayo ,Dhobleey , Bardera , Luuq , Dolow and Afmadow, the Company has over 45 Casual and permanent workers and several casual laborers as need may rise.

Our Vision

Create highly skilled business opportunities for Somali business workers, technical workforce, and construction with the best standards and availability of quality goods and services for our customers.

Our mission is to foster corporate, governmental, and non-governmental relationships to support economic, health, and education systems development. We deliver relief and development programs to create a sustainable state and strive to meet customer expectations through high-quality service and timely delivery.
